My Brand

When twins are born, the "A" twin and the "B" twin are labeled as "A" or "B" on papers. Jill was labeled as the "B" twin and born with schizophrenia, diagnosed by the age of 20. Schizophrenia affects 1 in 10 individuals worldwide and is only a miniscule of the demographics, leaving out people with bipolar, depression, anxiety, etc. This brand is the culture that advocates for mental illness awareness; breaking stereotypes and stigma around mental illness.
